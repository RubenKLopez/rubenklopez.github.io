Steps in Our Works

We work with every customer to create their unique and delicious
custom cake - all made from scratch with wholesome all-natural ingredients.

Collect Order Details

Custom made to your needs - we do our very best to achieve the look and taste described in your order.

Bake the Layers

Whether it be white cake or chocolate cake, our cake batter is made fresh, and baked until puffed and fluffy.

Fill & Frost

Depending on your order, silky buttercream, chocolate fudge or freshly sliced fruit is filled between layers, and a delicious frosting is applied all around.

Decorate & Finalize Product

Our signature decorations and attention to detail leave our cakes with a distinguished and attention-grabbing finish.
